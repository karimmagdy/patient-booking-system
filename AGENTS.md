## What this app is
Build a no-auth booking system for a clinic. Patient visits the site, sees available 60-min slots (Sun-Thu 4-9pm), picks one, enters name+phone, books. Gets a confirmation page. Clinic owner can open /admin to see all bookings in a table. No payment, no login. Supabase stores slots and bookings with wide-open RLS (app is internal + public booking). Seed 25 slots across 5 working days.
